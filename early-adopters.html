NoFilter's Early Adopters Group

NoFilter is an app for Travel Photographers. It shows you the best photo spots around the world.
We are new (3 months of life), so, we are creating an Early Adopter group, meaning, the first group of users to join the app.

Why are we creating an Early Adopters group?

Because we need:

  • Get feedback from you, to improve the app.
  • Break “the Chicken or the Egg” problem. We need more photo spots to get more “regular users” and create a big community.

Do you want to join us?

You can:

  • Download the App (iOS or Android) and upload your own spots
  • Join the Early Adopter group mailing list (NO SPAM. We’ll send you just a short survey)

How do you benefit from this?

  • If you loves to travel and photography, NoFilter is a really nice app for you. Check more details in our home page
  • More people is going to follow you on Instagram, or your personal website.
  • The Early Adopters group is the “User Suggested” list in the app. So, you’ll boost your followers flow.
  • The app is going to be free, for ever. But, if we add a premium feature in the future, you’ll get it for free.

The faster we get content, the faster we’ll created the community.
You can also publish this link (https://getnofilter.com/early-adopters) in an Instagram Story, Twitter, or Facebook, so more users can join the group.

We have a huge list of features that we are going to add in the followings weeks.
Before adding any new feature, we’ll send an email to the Early Adopters group, with a survey, so you can vote which of the new features you think you are going to enjoy more, so then we’ll implement that one.

See you there!
